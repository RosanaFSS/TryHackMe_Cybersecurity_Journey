<h2>Scenario overview</h2>
<p>Learn how to use SOC Simulator by completing your first scenario. Close all True Positive alerts to pass!</p>

<h3>Scenario overview</h3>
<p>Dive into the heat of a live phishing attack as it unfolds within the corporate network. In this high-pressure scenario, your role is to meticulously analyse and document each phase of the breach as it happens.<BR>

Can you piece together the attack chain in real-time and prepare a comprehensive report on the malicious activities?</p>

<h3>Scenario objectives</h3>
<p>

- Monitor and analyse real-time alerts as the attack unfolds.<br>
- Identify and document critical events such as PowerShell executions, reverse shell connections, and suspicious DNS requests.<br>
- Create detailed case reports based on your observations to help the team understand the full scope of the breach.</p>
